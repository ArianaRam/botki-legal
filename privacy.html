Privacy Policy — Botki

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Botki is a personal automation tool developed and operated by an individual developer. It generates short video content and posts it to TikTok accounts owned and controlled by the developer.

What data we access

When a TikTok account is connected to Botki, we request only the minimum data needed to operate: basic profile information (such as display name and avatar) and the ability to upload and publish video content to that account, via TikTok's official Content Posting API.

How we use it

Data obtained through TikTok's API is used solely to authenticate the connected account and to publish video content on its behalf, as configured by the account owner. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to posting content.

What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok account data with third parties. We do not use TikTok data to build advertising profiles. We do not access data beyond what is required for posting functionality.

Data storage

Access tokens and account identifiers are stored securely and are retained only for as long as the integration remains active. They are used solely to maintain the connection between Botki and the authorized TikTok account.

Data deletion

You may request deletion of any stored data associated with your TikTok account at any time by contacting us at the email below. Disconnecting the TikTok account within TikTok's own app settings also revokes Botki's access.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
